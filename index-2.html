What We Teach CodeVentures .

A student-run non-profit aimed at teaching younger children basic python skills, its applications, and its advantages over other languages while also providing insight into artificial intelligence, especially with its concurrency in the modern-day world. Codeventures seeks to find passionate young individuals interested in computer science, and develops a project at the intersection of this field to address real world problems.

About Us

Hi! Our names are Tristan, Rahul, Aadi, and Karthik, all high schoolers from the California Bay Area, and we are the creators of CodeVentures.

Ever since we were little, technology and innovation have been a shared passion of ours. Tristan loves developing websites and apps, Rahul enjoys diving into the world of machine learning, Aadi is fascinated by robotics and automation, and Karthik excels in cybersecurity and ethical hacking.

We decided to create CodeVentures because, similar to our own journey, we appreciate the hard work and commitment required to excel in the field of computer science while balancing academics and social priorities.

We want this platform to be a space where we can teach kids the basics of coding and help them better understand AI. We also hope to inspire curiosity and creativity, encouraging our students to think critically and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. Tune in for future projects and exciting developments for CodeVentures!
