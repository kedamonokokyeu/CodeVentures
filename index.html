Codeventures Curriculum

Codeventures seeks to find passionate young individuals interested in computer science, and develops a project at the intersection of this field to address real world problems. No coding experience required.

  1. 1

    Registration

    As soon as possible

    You complete the application so that we are aware of who will be attending.

  2. 2

    Lessons

    ~2 months

    You will be lectured on various topics in computer science ranging from how computers work to different programming languages, and important current topics such as ethical use of AI from professional mentors, special guests, and students.

  3. 3

    Projects

    ~1-2 months during lessons

    With guidance from your mentors, you will complete your final project, culminating from the lessons and topics we cover.

  4. 4

    Presentation

    At the end of the summer

    All students in the Codeventures program will present their projects they have created.
